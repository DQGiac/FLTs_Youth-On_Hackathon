<h1>IDEA</h1>
Social science subjects, such as Geography, History, or Literature, might sometimes be hard to visualize and learn by heart.<br>
Therefore, we designed this website, to reshape the way we learn.<br>
<br>
<h1>FEATURE</h1>
- Mindmap Generator: Generates mind maps from documents or text<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;=> Condense lessons into a readable, easy-to-remember format.<br>
- AI Storytelling: Generates voice and images from documents or text<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;=> Increase lessons' .<br>
- AI Podcast: Generates audio from document and text<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;=> Prompt users to learn by listening while doing another task.<br>
